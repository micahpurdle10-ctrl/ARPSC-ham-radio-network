ARPSC HAMS WEB APP - QUICK START
================================================================================

LAUNCHING THE APP
-----------------

1. Double-click the "ARPSC-START.bat" file
2. A window will open automatically in your browser
3. If nothing opens, copy this into your browser address bar:
   file:///[path-to-folder]/WORKING-LAUNCHER.html

FIRST TIME LOGGING IN
---------------------

You need two things:
1. Your FULL NAME (required) - Example: John Davis
2. Your PIN (provided by app administrator)
3. Callsign (optional) - Your ham radio callsign like "N4XYZ"

Enter these and click "Login"

STAYING LOGGED IN
-----------------

Once logged in, the app remembers you even if you:
- Close the browser
- Restart your computer
- Shut down the browser completely

You stay logged in until you click the "Logout" button.

MAIN FEATURES
-------------

HOME: County status and your role info
WEATHER: Real-time radar and storm alerts
CHAT: Multi-room messaging with other app users
MEETINGS: County meetings and events
CHECK-IN: Report your status and location
FREQUENCIES: Macomb County repeater frequencies
SETTINGS: Theme and admin features (if Moderator/Owner)

USING THE WEATHER RADAR
-----------------------

Click the WEATHER tab to see:
- Live radar with rain/snow colors
- Storm intensity map
- Click the PLAY button (▶) to animate forecast
- Use menu (≡) button for different weather layers

USING CHAT
----------

Click CHAT tab to:
- Select a room from the dropdown (General, Weather, Emergency, etc.)
- See messages from other logged-in users
- Type a message and hit Send
- Messages only go to users with the app open

EMERGENCY ALERTS (Moderators/Owners only)
-----------------------------------------

Click HOME tab → "Send Emergency Alert" button
Alert posts to the Emergency Room and shows to all logged-in users

LOGGING OUT
-----------

Click the "LOGOUT" button in the top right
Confirm when asked
You'll be logged out and must log in again next time

TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------

PROBLEM: Blank page when app opens
SOLUTION: 
  - Refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
  - Close and re-run ARPSC-START.bat
  - Try a different browser (Chrome vs Edge)

PROBLEM: "Invalid PIN" message
SOLUTION:
  - Check PIN from app administrator
  - Make sure CAPS LOCK isn't on
  - After 3 failed tries, account locks until next day
  - Ask Moderator/Owner to unlock in SETTINGS

PROBLEM: Weather radar not showing
SOLUTION:
  - Check internet connection
  - Radar only works with active internet
  - Refresh page

PROBLEM: Can't see other users' messages
SOLUTION:
  - They must be logged in to the app
  - You must be in the same chat room
  - Refresh the chat (go to different room then back)

DARK MODE
---------

Toggle "Dark" checkbox in the header for dark theme
